Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 28, 2024

1. Information We Access

Our application accesses the following information from your Google account:

  • Email Address: To identify you and send emails from your account
  • Gmail Send Permission: To send emails on your behalf
  • Basic Profile Information: Your name and profile picture for display purposes

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Authenticate your identity
  • Send emails from your Gmail account to recipients you specify
  • Display your name and email in the application interface

3. Data Storage and Security

  • No Permanent Storage: We do not permanently store your emails, contacts, or personal data
  • Session-Based: Your authentication tokens are stored only during your active session
  • Local Processing: Email lists and attachments are processed locally and deleted after sending
  • No Third-Party Sharing: We never share your data with third parties

4. Data Retention

We retain your data only for the duration of your active session. When you:

  • Log out of the application
  • Close your browser
  • Session expires

All your data is immediately deleted from our servers.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke access to your Google account at any time
  • Request deletion of any stored session data
  • Contact us with privacy concerns
